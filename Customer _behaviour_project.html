Sales Performance Analysis

Power BI • SQL • Excel

This project analyzes sales performance data to uncover trends in revenue, customer behavior, and product performance. The goal was to provide actionable insights using interactive dashboards.

Objectives

  • Analyze revenue trends across time
  • Identify top-performing products
  • Segment customers based on behavior
  • Support data-driven business decisions

Tools Used

  • Power BI – Dashboards & visualizations
  • SQL – Data querying & aggregation
  • Excel – Data cleaning & preparation

Key Insights

  • Top products generated over 60% of total revenue
  • Returning customers had higher average order value
  • Seasonal trends significantly affected sales
